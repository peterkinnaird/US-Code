The following is a list of sections of the Revised Statutes of the United States of 1878 showing their classification to the United States Code.

Abbreviations

Rep.—Repealed. Where ``Rep.'' appears in the status column, information about the Rev. St. section can often be found under the U.S.C. title and section or in a prior provisions note under a nearby section. Where no U.S.C. reference appears, the Rev. St. section was repealed before the adoption of the United States Code (1926) or for other reasons was not included in the Code but was subsequently repealed.

Elim.—Eliminated. Where ``Elim.'' appears in the status column, information about the Rev. St. section can often be found under the U.S.C. title and section or in a prior provisions note under a nearby section.

Rev. T.—Revised Title. Where ``Rev. T.'' appears in the status column, the U.S.C. reference is to the title and section where the Rev. St. section was classified before the title was revised. For the disposition of sections of the former title as a result of a positive law codification, see Table I.

I.R.C. '39—Internal Revenue Code of 1939. Where ``I.R.C. '39'' appears in the status column, it is an indication that the Rev. St. section was repealed or otherwise superseded by the Internal Revenue Code of 1939 or an earlier revenue act.
